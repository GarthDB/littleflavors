--- layout: dish-recipe title: Stir fry image: /img/Vegs_stir_fry.jpg description : Stir frying is a Chinese cooking technique in which ingredients are fried in a small amount of very hot oil while being stirred in a wok. ingredients : Ginger grated - 1
Green chillies sliced - 2
Baby corn 8 nos
Carrot small 3 nos
Red green yellow capsicum 1/2 each
Mushroom fresh and sliced 8 nos
Honey 1 tsp
Corn flour 1 tbsp
Vinegar 1/2 tsp
Cauliflower 8 florets
Broccoli 8 florets
Chilli flakes 1 tsp
Thyme 1/2 tsp
Oregano 1/2 tsp
Pepper pwd 1/2 tsp
Salt as per taste
Olive oil 4 tbsp
Water 1/2 to 1 cup method: Wash and boil mushrooms for 1 min Cauliflower and Broccoli for 2-3 mins separately and throw away the water .
Cut capsicum corn long strips .French bean cut into long strips diagonally
Take olive oil in a thick bottom pan or kadai add ginger chillies add the mushrooms carrots corn capsicum boiled cauliflower and broccoli .add thyme and oregano.Add salt as desired .
Mix pepper cornflour 1/2 cup water soya sauce and honey in a separate bowl then add it to the veggies let it cook till done. Add more water if required .Cook and stir for a while
Serve piping hot with fried rice or as a meal by itself ---
